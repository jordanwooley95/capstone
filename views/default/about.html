About Joint Ventures and the team


Who We Are:

Joint Ventures is a cannabis dispensary that provides a pathway between nationally recognized cannabis based brands and customers. Annually, we host several events in which we invite several collaborative brands. During these events, we allow these brands the ability to market and sell their products.

Based on the results of sales as well as the connections built with our clientele, these brands then have the opportunity to become an official vendor for Joint Ventures, allowing us access to sell their products within our dispensary. This joint collaboration allows clients to purchase products that are not readily available within their area, as well as allows brands to expand their clientele nationally.
